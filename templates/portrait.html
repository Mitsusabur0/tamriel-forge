Painters' Atelier

When memory needs a face,

the artists of this house will set it upon canvas.

These painters work in ash tones, moonlit silvers, battle reds, and royal golds. Give them a hero, and they will decide whether the world should meet a saint, a sellsword, or a storm with boots on.

Notes from the studio

One canvas catches the hero at their most iconic, the other tests a different mood, light, or posture. The painters keep both at hand until you decide which likeness deserves to travel the realm.
